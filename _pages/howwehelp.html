Naw Heh Say Gaul

I am Naw Heh Say Gaul, one of the church music educators at the Church Music Department of the Karen Baptist Theological Seminary (KBTS). The seminary is located in Insein, Yangon, Myanmar. I finished my Certificate of Church Music in 2013, and Diploma of Church Music in 2014 from KBTS. The Church Music Department was launched in 2003, and since then, Friends of Burma supported the department with a number of books and instruments from time to time till now.

Furthermore, FOB partially supported two music educators from the department, to study at the Philippines Baptist Theological Seminary and Church Music School, Singapore Bible College several years ago. Even now, FOB partially sponsors one of music educators who is studying Master of Church Music (2020-2022) at Church Music School, Singapore Bible College.
